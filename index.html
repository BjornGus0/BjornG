Björn Gustafsson

About me

Björn Gustafsson is a composer and performer from Northern Virginia. Throughout their musical training they have constantly strived to tell a story or describe an event, with their most recent works focusing on describing the beauty of nature through sound. Their music spans a wide range of topics and styles; incorporating a multitude of different playing techniques into their music and performance to ‘expand the color palette’ of every instrument.
